# NEA AI Brand Assets

NEA AI uses a compact control-plane mark derived from two roots:

- The NEA Brain and NEA Flow visual language: cyan/yellow neon lines, circuit
  nodes, and connected agent paths.
- The Colombian, Medellin use of "nea": a barrio-born expression of identity,
  resilience, friendship, and streetwise confidence.

The brand should feel local-first and technical without becoming a caricature.
Use urban energy through contrast, glow, and connected paths rather than
stereotyped characters or costumes.

## Files

- `nea-ai-logo.svg` - horizontal lockup for README, docs, and release pages.
- `nea-ai-mark.svg` - standalone NEA mark for square placements.
- `nea-ai-icon.svg` - dark square app/repository icon.

## Palette

- Cyan: `#2DE2E6`
- Mint: `#A8FFCB`
- Yellow: `#F9F871`
- Ink: `#071114`
- Slate: `#122126`

Use the SVG files directly when possible. Raster exports should be generated
from these sources so the brand stays consistent.
